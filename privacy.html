Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025‑08‑18

This Policy describes how the App handles data.

  1. Data the App processes. OAuth tokens from TikTok (access/refresh), video files you choose locally, and metadata (titles, schedule times). Tokens are stored locally on your machine.
  2. No analytics / no sharing. The App does not send your data to third parties except TikTok APIs to perform uploads.
  3. Retention. Tokens and settings remain on your device until you delete them. Videos are read from your chosen folder; the App does not keep copies after upload.
  4. Security. Keep your machine and TikTok credentials secure. Revoke tokens in TikTok if compromised.
  5. Your rights. Since data stays locally, removal is done by deleting local files/settings and revoking tokens.
  6. Contact. [your email].
